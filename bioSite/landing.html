Roy Gotzman

Biography

Roy Gotzman was born in Springfield, Oregon December 3rd, 1960. He received his bachelors in Christian Education in 1988. He married Alaina Peterson in the summer of 1983. He and his wife have been married thirty eight years. They have 12 children (four biological and 8 adopted). His children are Kenner, Anjuleah, Kyle, Olivia, Jesse, Micah, Sarah, Hannah, Isaac, Timothy, Malachi, and Noah. Roy started his career in education and Christian ministries. He worked as a youth minister, interim pastor, and Educational Supervisor for the first few years out of high school. He then transitioned into asbestos and lead based paint abatement. He worked for his father in law for seven years before co-founding his own asbestos removal company. He continued to work in asbestos abatement for an additional fifteen years before selling the company. He then started a business with his wife and made goat milk soap that is sold across the Pacific Northwest in small gift shops and farmers markets. On top of running the farm he works and volunteers at the local church.
